Lasagna

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta.

Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish.

Is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 2tbs olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 large bell pepper
  • 28-ounce tomato sauce
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper and onions, add back the beef
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
