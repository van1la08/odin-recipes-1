Steak

This Pan-Seared Steak has a garlic rosemary-infused butter that makes it taste steakhouse quality

You’ll be impressed at how easy it is to make the perfect steak – seared and caramelized on the outside, and so juicy inside.

This 20-minute recipe is done on the stovetop in one pan (no need to finish it in the oven). This is one of our favorite steak recipes and we make it year-round because it’s such a quick and convenient cooking method. That garlic butter is lip-smacking good!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound steak
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Steps:

  1. Pat dry
  2. Season generously
  3. Preheat the pan
  4. Sear steaks
  5. Add butter and aromatics
  6. Remove steak
